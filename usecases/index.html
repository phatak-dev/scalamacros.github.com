Recently we have published a paper covering the state of the art of macrology in Scala 2.10. It provides an interesting perspective and an up-to-date interpretation of some of the use cases below, most of which were written before 2.10.

NEW Our new talk "What Are Macros Good For?" describes and systemizes uses that macros found among Scala 2.10 users. The thesis of the talk is that macros are good for code generation, static checking and DSLs, illustrated with a number of examples from research and industry.
